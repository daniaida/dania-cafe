My Favorite Cafes

VCR Ritchie

VCR Ritchie offers a delightful combination of sweet and savory treats. Their waffle with ice cream is a must-try—crispy, fluffy, and perfectly paired with creamy ice cream. I also tried their chocolate cake, and it was absolutely divine—rich, moist, and full of chocolatey goodness.

Cafe Chef Wan

As expected from a cafe by the renowned Chef Wan, the food here is nothing short of amazing. Their cucur udang stands out as the best I’ve ever had—crispy, flavorful, and perfectly complemented by their dipping sauce. Truly a taste you won’t forget!

Brew & Bread

I celebrated my birthday at Brew & Bread, and it was such a memorable experience. The cake and drinks were delicious, and the meal was equally impressive (though I forgot to take pictures!). A cozy and welcoming spot for any special occasion.

Blackbixon Cafe & Restaurant

Blackbixon is a haven for food lovers. Their tiramisu is simply exquisite—light, creamy, and with the perfect coffee kick. The pasta and burger? Absolute perfection! Both dishes are packed with flavor and leave a lasting impression.

Pokok

Pokok is a dessert lover’s paradise. Their sweet treats are outstanding, but the savory options are just as impressive. The pesto pasta is flavorful and fresh, while their fish & chips is perfectly crispy and satisfying. A well-rounded menu that doesn’t disappoint.
